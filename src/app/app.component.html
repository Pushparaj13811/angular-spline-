About Earth | By - Pushparaj

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It has a diverse environment with various ecosystems, including forests, deserts, oceans, and polar regions. With a diameter of about 12,742 km, Earth is the fifth largest planet in the solar system. Its atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen, which are essential for life. Earth also experiences a variety of natural phenomena such as weather, seasons, and geological activities like earthquakes and volcanic eruptions.
